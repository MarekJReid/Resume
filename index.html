Education

RMIT University - Melbourne Campus

BA - Fine Arts - Contemporary Photoraphy • April 2007

During my BA I took all of the skills that I had learned from my work as a Commercial Photographer and begun exploring my artistic aspirations. Having grown tired expressing the ideals of large corporations, I began to develop my own voice. This degree helped me build an artistic reputation within the Australian Contemporary Art scene and exposed me to many new and exciting ideas and concepts. It also gave me the courage and motivation to begin exhibiting my final works and I went on to have several solo exhibitions.

Marek J. Reid Photography

Freelance Photographer / Social Media Manager • March 2013 - Present

This is a role began as my side project whilst at University. I specialized in Food/Restaurant Photography and Portraiture Photography. Whilst photographing many small restaurants in Melbourne I was approached to manage their social media portfolios as there was little knowledge on how to do so. I now manage the social media presence of 5 restaurants remotely. This includes photography, content creation and CMS, EMS, Analytics and Social Media Marketing strategies and Web Development. This business adapts the needs of each individual client and situation from a website for a hole in the wall cafe to a fleet of burger trucks and fine dining restaurants. This is a role I have continued in a freelance/consultancy capacity since leaving to travel the world 6 months ago.
